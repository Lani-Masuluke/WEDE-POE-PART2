Sell or Donate your Items

Tired of your old grandmother's coat? Let us give your item a new life. sell or exercise kindness!

Here is how to do it

  1. Log in to your RetroGems Imagined site

  2. Click on Sell or Donate Page on the Navigation bar

  3. Fill in the form

  4. Select sell or donate

  5. Select the condition of the item

  6. Add price, R0 if you are donating

  7. Upload picture of item

  8. You can also add additional details of the item you wish to sell or donate

  9. Submit
